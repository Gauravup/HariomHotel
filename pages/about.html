Our Story

A Legacy of Taste & Tradition

Founded in 2014 by the Sharma family, HariOM Hotel began as a humble eatery with a dream — to bring authentic Indian flavors to every table with the warmth of home cooking and the elegance of fine dining.

Over a decade later, we've grown into one of the city's most beloved dining destinations, serving over 5,000 happy guests every month. Our kitchen blends time-honored recipes with modern culinary techniques, creating an experience that honors tradition while embracing innovation.

Our Mission

To deliver an extraordinary dining experience that combines authentic flavors, premium ingredients, and heartfelt hospitality — making every visit a cherished memory for our guests.

Our Vision

To become India's most loved luxury dining brand, recognized for culinary excellence, sustainable practices, and creating a space where food brings people together in joy and celebration.

Our Values

Quality, integrity, innovation, and community. We believe great food has the power to transform moments into memories and strangers into family.

Our Journey

A Decade of Excellence

2014

The Beginning

HariOM Hotel opens its doors with a 20-seat restaurant and a menu of 15 dishes crafted from family recipes.

2016

First Award

Awarded "Best New Restaurant" by the City Food Guide. Menu expanded to 50+ items.

2018

Expansion

Renovated and expanded to 80 seats. Launched catering services and private dining room.

2020

Going Digital

Launched online ordering platform. Served 10,000+ orders during the pandemic through delivery.

2022

Luxury Upgrade

Complete interior redesign. Introduced Chef's Tasting Menu and premium dining experience.

2025

Today

Serving 5,000+ guests monthly with 100+ menu items and a team of 40+ passionate professionals.
